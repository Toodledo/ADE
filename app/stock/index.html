A simple directive that makes a stock editable.

Options:

Movement option "0": (only price is shown)

Movement option "1": (price and movement displayed inline)

Movement option "2": (price is shown, movement is shown in a popup)

The directive will broadcast a message when the edit starts and when it finishes so that your controllers and models can handle the edit properly.
